The Purpose of Taking a Coach Approach

Taking a coach approach means believing and acting in a way that supports people to thrive and reach their fullest potential. This means supporting people to try new things, to reflect on what’s working and what’s not, and to imagine what’s possible.

The purpose of coaching isn’t to solve other people’s problems or fix them. The people you coach are experts on their own work and life. They’re best equipped to find solutions that work for them.

The goal is to work alongside people to understand what success means to them, what’s important, what actions will move them towards their job-specific or career development goals, and what might be getting in the way. Your role is to support them in setting goals, creating new habits, and embedding them in day-to-day activities so they stick.
