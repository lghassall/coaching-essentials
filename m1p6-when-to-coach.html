When to Coach (and when not to!)

Communication falls along a spectrum, from telling people what to do (directive) to working with others to find their own solutions (non-directive).

When you have a challenge and approach someone for help, what do you prefer: the direct approach (just tell me what to do!) or a coaching approach (work with me to find solutions)? You might say: It depends on the situation.

It's important to recognize that coaching isn't a one-size-fits-all approach. You need to take a minute to think and decide whether it makes more sense to take a coaching approach, a directive approach, or something in between. There’s no right or wrong, but some approaches will be more effective than others.
