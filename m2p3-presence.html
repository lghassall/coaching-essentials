Presence

Presence is an ability and willingness to be deeply engaged.

"Your presence is the most precious gift you can give to another human being."

Marshall B. Rosenberg

In our busy world, presence and active listening have become challenging as we deal with more priorities and distractions. People are feeling disconnected.

Think about a time when you felt that someone was present and really listened to you. What did that feel like? How did that impact your connection with that person?

Our brains naturally reflect the emotions and actions around us. When we feel understood and valued, we mirror that back, which creates deeper connection and understanding with that person.
