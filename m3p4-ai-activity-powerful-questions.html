Optional Activity: Using AI to practice asking powerful questions

This activity offers a low-risk space to practice asking and refining powerful questions using Microsoft CoPilot, a generative artificial intelligence (gen AI) tool.

AI can make mistakes. This activity is a helpful way to experiment and build confidence, but it’s not a substitute for real coaching with real people, where nuance, emotion, and genuine connection matter most. Treat suggestions as starting points for reflection and refer to the course content for the most accurate information. BCPS employees must not enter any confidential or personal information into publicly available generative AI tools, such as ChatGPT. For more information, review the policy on the use of generative AI.

Activity

Instructions

  1. Open Microsoft Copilot on your government workstation. Note: For your best learning, create the questions yourself even after reading the tips and suggested questions. Use the tips as a guide to create your next question.
  2. Click the Copy text button to copy the text from the box below. Then, go to Microsoft 365 Copilot and paste it using Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on a Mac).

Tips to deepen your learning with CoPilot Chat:

  • Create your own prompts to get more practice asking powerful questions.
  • Give it different scenarios, for example, someone who is challenging.
