Introduction

Using a coaching model can help you focus your conversations, explore challenges, and identify practical next steps. We use the GROW Model because it’s simple, easy to remember, AND it works!

The model’s letters – GROW – stand for Goal, Reality, Options, and Will. Even though the GROW Model starts with G and ends with W, it’s not linear so you can bounce between them as needed.

GOAL

An intentional coaching conversation starts with a GOAL Powerful questions are used to understand what the focus is for the conversation. We want to understand why it’s important to the person and what they want to walk away from the conversation with.

Actionable Insight

Ask questions like:

  • What do you need right now?
  • What’s important about that right now?
  • What do you want to take away from our conversation?
  • How will we know you have what you need from this conversation?

REALITY

Once we are clear on what’s important to the other person to get out of the conversation, we explore the REALITY or current situation. We ask questions to find out what’s happening for the person and how they think and feel about it.

Actionable Insight

Ask questions like:

  • What’s happening right now?
  • What have you already tried?
  • What challenges are you experiencing?
  • What’s within your control, and what isn’t?

OPTIONS

When the other person is ready to consider OPTIONS, we ask questions to explore what’s possible. We find out what they’ve tried, what’s worked for others, and how they want it to turn out.

Actionable Insight

Ask questions like:

  • What options do you see?
  • What are the pros and cons of each option?
  • If there were no constraints, what might you do?
  • What might someone else recommend you do?

WILL

When we have a few options, we move onto WILL. This is when we ask questions to determine what they will commit to and what they will do when faced with obstacles.

Actionable Insight

Ask questions like:

  • What will you do next??
  • When will you take this action?
  • Who or what could support you?
  • How will you know you’re successful?

BCPS Corporate Values

The GROW model supports the BCPS corporate value of courage by encouraging others to take thoughtful risks, take initiative, and look beyond the process to see the possible.
