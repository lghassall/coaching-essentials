Optional Activity: Pulling it all together

This activity offers a low-risk space pull all your coaching skills together and practice in a coaching simulation using Microsoft CoPilot, a generative artificial intelligence (gen AI) tool.

It's a helpful way to experiment and build confidence, but it’s not a substitute for coaching with real people. AI can make mistakes. Treat suggestions as a starting point for reflection and refer to the course content for the most accurate information.

Alert! Copilot doesn't access external or personal information unless you share it, and it never stores sensitive information without your consent. All responses are generated based on your input and Copilot’s built-in knowledge base. BCPS employees must not enter any confidential or personal information into publicly available generative AI tools, such as ChatGPT. For more information, review the policy on the use of generative AI.

Activity

Instructions

  1. Open Microsoft Copilot on your government workstation.
  2. Select the Copy text button to copy the text from the box below. Then, go to Microsoft 365 Copilot and paste it using Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on a Mac).

Deepen your learning by asking Copilot to:

  • Adjust the tone...
    • “Be mildly resistant.”
    • “Be defensive and skeptical about coaching.”
    • “Be emotionally withdrawn at first but open up later.”
  • Or change the focus...
    • “You’re struggling with work-life balance.”
    • “You’re facing a conflict with your manager.”
    • “You’re unclear about your long-term career goals.”
