Preparing for Part 2: Virtual Skills Lab

What is it?

Part 2 of Coaching Essentials is a 3-hour virtual practice session lead by a certified coach in MS Teams. This is an opportunity to practice your coaching skills in a safe environment with coaching experts.

Why's it important?

It’s one thing to learn skills and practice them in simulations, and it’s another to practice with another real-life person. Practicing coaching skills ensures that the hard work you put into learning these skills actually pays off!

“The coaching practice was very helpful. I didn't feel like doing it at the start, but trusting the process was helpful. It was hard to wind myself up for, but in the end, it was the most valuable part of the day.”
– Learner feedback

What can I expect?

After a demonstration by the coach facilitator(s), you'll join a virtual breakout room with two or three other learners. You’ll each take turns being the coach, the person being coached, and the observer. Before the session, you'll need to come up with two topics to be coached on. Sample topics for coaching practice can be found under Resources. A facilitator will drop into your breakout rooms to observe and support if needed. After you practice, you’ll return to debrief with the rest of the group.

How can I prepare?

Complete the readiness checklist below before attending the virtual skills lab. If you’re not able to check each box off, review sections of the eLearning or consider signing up for a later session. This will help ensure that everyone gets the the most out of it.

“As a shy and introverted person, finding out about the coaching practice was both a blessing and a curse. I debated dropping out of the course to avoid it. I am glad I decided against dropping out, as the session was quite helpful and the coaching practice went well!”
– Learner feedback
